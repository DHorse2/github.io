Who is Axion?
Harvey Horsman: Founder of Axion
Axion is owned and operated by Harvey Horsman of Vancouver, BC.  Harvey and his wife Audrey have three adult children (who thankfully left the nest some time ago). They continue to live in Vancouver with their cat Blaze, a truly loyal, though possessed, feline of great beauty.
Harvey's IT career spans over thirty years. Many changes in methods and technology have occurred, and Axion has kept abreast of these emerging changes in CATV services and operations.
It has always been Harvey's focus on customer service and sound management that are the key to Axion's success.
