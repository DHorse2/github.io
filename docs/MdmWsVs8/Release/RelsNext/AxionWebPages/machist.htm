About Axion
Axion's history...

Axion was founded by Harvery and Bob Connaroe in the early 1980's to provide services to the CATV industry.  Their vision was to always keep their focus on a single high quality product within the CATV industry.

Harvey, Bob, and several other talented individuals made contributions to the KBil product. Bob eventually moved on to other adventures, however his technical brillance and fore-sight within the IT industry are still an influence today.
