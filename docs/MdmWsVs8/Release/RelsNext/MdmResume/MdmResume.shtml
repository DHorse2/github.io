Portfolio work will be available on this site

In 2008 I found myself in the uncomfortable position that my portfolio could only be drawn from of other compainies' proprietary software. Good bragging rights but not something you can actually show anyone...

In addition, the prototypes, study and research projects I did have available were not very impressive in my own view. The projects that remained that were both complete and commercially used were all tools and utilities and written on the multivalued platforms. I decided that the main goals of my upgrading for C# and web enabling applications would have to include some significant work that I could distribute however I chose.

As I add content to this site I will be including much of the work in the form of a portfolio. Keep in mind that although the demonstrated skills don't focus on business applications, that has been the area I have been most active.

During this site construction phase please feel free to contact me if your needs are specialized enough to require one of the utilities that appear here. None are open source or in the form of downloadable installs at this time so just send me an email.
